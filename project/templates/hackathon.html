DUCSS Hackathon sponsored by for


Ever wanted to hack on something meaningful? Want to earn fantastic prizes while doing it?

DUCSS, sponsored by Guidewire and in association with SpunOut.ie are holding a hackathon in Trinity Hall, November 22nd from 10AM. The theme is supporting mental health, and you, together with your team, will get an opportunity to work on something meaningful that ties together mental health awareness and education with the modern world of technology and the internet!


There are some great prizes up for grabs with €50 Amazon vouchers going to each member of the winning team. Every participant with also leave with a Raspberry Pi so there's no reason not to sign up!

Make sure to do it quick as places are limited!




Sign up now!

WHERE?

The hackathon will be taking place in the canteen in Trinity Hall on Saturday November 22nd from 10AM.


Trinity Hall is located a short bus ride away from the City Centre campus. The 140 bus from Nassau Street will take you opposite the main gates.

PRIZES?

We've got some fantastic prizes lined up for our winners! Each member of the winning team will be going home with €50 Amazon vouchers in their pockets!


Just because you don't win doesn't mean you leave empty-handed however, as all participants will be getting a Raspberry Pi kit. If that's not reason enough to enter, we don't know what is!

MENTAL HEALTH?

SpunOut.ie is Ireland's leading website created by young people for young people. SpunOut offer thousands of pages containing advice on topics such as mental health, sexual health, drugs, equality and politics.


SpunOut, having a large web presence are an example of a charity embracing modern technology. The aim of the hackathon is to create new and innovative technologies to help young people in the modern digital world.

Please note: Only Trinity College Dublin students may enter and active participation is required to earn a Raspberry Pi.
